Book your consultation

I am a licensed therapist offering individual and group therapy sessions that are designed to enhance self-awareness through creative exploration. My approach is centered on body-centered psychotherapy and focuses on imparting coping skills to support individuals in their journey towards personal growth.

During our conversation, you will have the chance to ask any questions you may have and get to know me a little better. I believe that therapy is a collaborative process and it is important to establish trust and rapport with your therapist. If you are interested in learning more about how I can support you, I encourage you to reach out to schedule a consultation. I am here to listen and help you on your journey towards personal growth and well-being.

If you are interested in scheduling a free consultation, please follow the link to my online calendar. Our consultation will last approximately 15-20 minutes and provide an opportunity to determine if working together in therapy would be a suitable fit.
