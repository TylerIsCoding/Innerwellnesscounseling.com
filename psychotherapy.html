Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy sessions last between 45-53 minutes and are reimbursed by insurance. If I currently do not accept your insurance learn more about out of network billing here.

I use psycho-dynamic theory as a foundation for the work I do with clients. If you are seeking to look more closely at the patterns and roots of your thoughts and behaviors this may be the therapeutic approach for you. I strive to meet you where you are by asking questions and giving feedback that inspires a deeper understanding of yourself and your relationships.

Ultimately, the goal of psychotherapy is to help the individual gain insight into their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, and to develop the skills and resources they need to lead a fulfilling and satisfying life. With the help of a trained mental health professional, psychotherapy can be an effective tool for addressing a wide range of emotional and psychological difficulties.
