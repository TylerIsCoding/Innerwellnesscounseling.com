FAQ

I know you might have questions about my movement and dance therapy services, and I'm here to provide answers. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions I receive from my clients. If you can't find the information you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact me. I'm happy to help!

How often should we meet?

Initially, I recommended we meet weekly for at least the first 3 months. This is an opportunity for us to build the foundation of our relationship, get to know each other, and develop therapeutic goals for our time together.

Do I have to have dance experience to try Dance/Movement Therapy?

You do not. Dance/Movement therapy is for everyone and no prior dance experience is necessary.

What can I expect from therapy with you, especially if I've never been to therapy before?

My goal is to provide a safe and comfortable space where you can freely express yourself. During our initial sessions, I prioritize listening to your concerns. As we progress, many of my clients find me to be an engaging and relatable therapist. I often use practical examples, psycho-education, and humor to support you throughout your therapeutic journey.

Where is your office located? Can you help me with directions?

I meet with individuals primarily on telehealth. I offer in-person sessions on Thursdays in downtown Boston.
