Psychotherapy Session

Psychotherapy is a mental health treatment that involves talking with a trained therapist to help resolve emotional and psychological issues. It provides a supportive environment to gain insight, develop coping skills, and achieve greater well-being. Psychotherapy can help you lead a more fulfilling life.

Authentic Movement Session

Movement therapy is a form of therapy that uses physical movement to improve physical, emotional, and mental well-being. It can involve activities such as dance, yoga, or Tai Chi, and is used to help individuals improve their physical coordination, relieve stress, and increase their self-awareness. Whether you're looking to manage physical pain or improve your emotional state, movement therapy can be an effective and enjoyable way to improve your overall health and quality of life.

Learn More

BC-DMT Licensure Supervision

BC-DMT (Board Certified Dance/Movement Therapist) licensure supervision is a professional process in which a qualified and experienced BC-DMT provides guidance and support to a therapist who is seeking to become licensed in the field of dance/movement therapy. This supervision helps the therapist gain the necessary skills and competencies to practice dance/movement therapy in a safe, ethical, and effective manner, and prepares them for the BC-DMT licensure exam. BC-DMT licensure supervision is a crucial step in the development of dance/movement therapists and helps to ensure that clients receive high-quality care.
